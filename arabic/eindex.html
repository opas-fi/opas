This multimedia course is based on "step by step Approach to reading and writing arabic" by Mohi e-Din Saleh. It assumes that the reader knows nothing about the arabic language.

It has ten lessons and every lesson should take no longer than an hour .

If you are a beginner, I recommend that you begin with Lesson One and progress through the course lesson by lesson. Just take your time and be sure to try all the problems.

After you finish this course you'll be ready to read the holy Quran 'In shaa ALLAH', as well as you will have the ability to write in arabic.
