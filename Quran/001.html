Al-Fatihah [The Opener]

  1. In the Name of Allah—the Most Compassionate, Most Merciful.
  2. All praise is for Allah—Lord of all worlds,[1]
    1. [1] i.e., Lord of everything in existence including angels, humans, and animals.
  3. the Most Compassionate, Most Merciful,
  4. Master of the Day of Judgment.
  5. You ˹alone˺ we worship and You ˹alone˺ we ask for help.
  6. Guide us along the Straight Path,
  7. the Path of those You have blessed—not those You are displeased with, or those who are astray.[1]
    1. [1] It is customary to say “Âmîn” or “Amen” at the end of this sûrah.
